Just an East Coast Girl out West

4 C's in Spokane, Washington 2024

The 4 C's, officially known as the Conference on College Composition and Communication (CCCC), was quite the adventure for a STEM major like me—who often won't crack open the textbook until the night before the exam (don't tell my professors 🤫). It was fascinating to dive into a world I rarely explore (and I’m already eyeing a return in 2025!). That said, I still haven’t figured out what 'abundance' means to rhet lit folks, despite it popping up over 500 times in the program—and even in the title of one of my own presentations! 😅.
